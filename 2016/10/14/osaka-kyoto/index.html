Osaka & Kyoto

14 October 2016 on Travel

Arrived at the second largest city in Japan, it is also a metropolitan area in Japan. Osaka (大阪).

大阪城 (Ōsakajō), Osaka Castle, the castle already reconstructed as a modern castle, it even has an elevator inside of it. Entrance fee: 600¥.

The famous Dōtonbori area, it is now a popular nightlife and entertainment area. Full of stuff, food, arcade game (I won a UFO catcher game!). You can spend your whole day in this area if you love shopping.

And we got to see Universal Studio in Halloween event!

The most amazing part in Universal Studio, Harry Potter! You can see a lot of Harry Potter property in this area, from candy, broomstick, hat, cape, castle, building, even a singing frog!

Tips: If you really want to ride that famous Harry Potter's attraction (Harry Potter 4D), come earlier, and go straight to queue up. We were late back then, and the queue was already 4 hour long wait.

06:00PM, and BOOM! Sudden explosion, and everything change, people start to run away. Zombies, butcher, crazy man with saw start to showing up. It was such an unforgettable experience.

And there were a lot of people with halloween costume!

A thousand year capital, formerly the Imperial capital of Japan for more than thousand years. Kyoto (京都).

There are a lot of scenery view in Kyoto, and most of it are temple.

You can rent a Yukata (浴衣) in Kyoto, almost in every famous scenery places. I think the price range from 3000¥-6000¥.

Arashiyama, is one of the popular destination in Kyoto. There are a lot of popular places inside this district, Bamboo Grooves, Okochi Sanso Villa (this one really worth it), Monkey Park, Tenryuji Temple, and more.

You can buy a Kyoto One Day pass ticket for bus and subway. Buy it if you have a lot of places to go. You can check the detail here.

Gion district, you can see Geisha here. If you want to see Geisha, walk around this area before 05:00PM, after that, many will go inside and entertain people. But there is also a Geisha show at Geisha Corner (located in this district).

If you have enough time, go travel to Kobe (神戸). Why?

This. Wagyu Beef. Cost above 5000¥, but really worth it.

If you have unspent money, and enough time, don't think too long.
